Vinagrete

The Best Side Dish In The World

A refreshing side dish to pair with chicken, pork, or beef.

Ingredients

  • 5 tomatoes, diced
  • ½ English cucumber, quartered and thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • ½ cup diced onion
  • ½ cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  • ¼ cup lime juice
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup diced green onions
  • ¼ cup finely chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, onion, parsley, lime juice, olive oil, green onions, cilantro, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Let stand at room temperature until flavors combine, about 3 hours.
